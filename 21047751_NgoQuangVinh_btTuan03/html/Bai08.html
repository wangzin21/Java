Syntax : border-color: (top) (right) (bottom) (left); Example: border-color: #85C226 #F7C200 # 4493AO #DF127B;

Here the top border is green(#85C226) in color, right border is yellow(#F7C200), the bottom has blue (#4493A0) and Left border is pink(DF127B) in color.

Here the top border (green) has a solid style, right (yellow) one has dotted, bottom (blue) one has groove and the left (pink) one has the double style.

Here Top (green) border's width is 5 pixel, right (yellow) border is 10 pixel wide, bottom (blue) is 15 pixel and left (pink) is 20 pixel wide.

No Border radius (0px) has been applied on the top-left (green-pink) corner. Border radius (30px) has been applied on the top-right (green-yellow) corner. Border radius (70px) has been applied on the bottom-right (blue-yellow) corner. Border radius (20px) has been applied on the bottom-left (blue-pink) corner.
